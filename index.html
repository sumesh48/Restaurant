ABOUT US

At Amma Biriyani , discover the healthy reasons to enjoy our delicious biriyani. Our cuisine is packed spices with abundance, explore your - self the unseen benefits of spices that bring a ray of good health to your life.
In 1984,Amma Biriyani opened its first outlet and changed the prevailing scenario forever. The Biriyani went solo, and made it silver, gold and platinum before we knew it. The industry was full of sceptics.
They thought wide variety was the key to success. They thought the future belonged to western/ junk/ fast food. But the quick success of Biriyani Hut made their doubt vanish and turned them believers of a new truth:
nothing satisfies a Keralaite like a good Biriyani. This realization sent a huge ripple through the food industry. We had set a trend and established a successful model.
